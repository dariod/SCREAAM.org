S C R E A A M

San Francisco’s Concerned Residents Experiencing Annoying Aircraft Maneuvers

We “San Francisco’s Concerned Residents Experiencing Annoying Aircraft Maneuvers” are an organized group that aims to eliminate or minimize unacceptable noise levels caused by aircraft from the San Francisco Airport (SFO) and the Oakland Airport (OAK) flying over San Francisco.

Get involved, help us

  1. Keep an eye on S.C.R.E.A.A.M. website for news and to see if any actions or help is needed. Alternatively, if you are really sick of the jet noise and want to help us more and get SUPER involved, then send us a message that says “I want to get SUPER INVOLVED!” and we will contact you directly.

  2. Follow the S.C.R.E.A.A.M. blog at the bottom of the page to get instant updates sent to your inbox about what is going on.

  3. Come to S.C.R.E.A.A.M. meetings when they happen. The dates will be posted on this site. Also, consider going to the broader SFO Airport/Community Roundtable meetings.

  4. Find a jet noise website you like (like http://stop.jetnoise.net) and start complaining or complain directly to these email addresses: sfo.noise@flysfo.com and 9-AWA-NoiseOmbudsman@faa.gov

  5. Ask neighbors that are affected by jet noise to get involved too.
